Frequently Asked Questions

How many photos should I upload?

+

We recommend 5-10 photos for the best results. Include the main living areas, bedrooms, kitchen, bathrooms, and any standout features. More photos allow for longer, more detailed videos.

What does the AI narrator say?

+

The AI generates a professional script based on the property details you provide (square footage, bedrooms, special features). It describes each room naturally, highlighting selling points in a warm, engaging tone.

Can I edit the narration script?

+

Yes! You can provide your own script or edit the AI-generated one before the video is created. Pro and Business plans include unlimited script revisions.

What video formats are supported?

+

We export in MP4 format with options for landscape (16:9 for YouTube/websites), square (1:1 for Instagram), and portrait (9:16 for TikTok/Reels). All exports include embedded subtitles.

How long does it take to create a video?

+

Most videos are ready in 2-5 minutes depending on length and complexity. Business plan users get priority processing for even faster turnaround.
